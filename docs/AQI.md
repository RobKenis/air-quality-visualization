# Air Quality Index (AQI)

[This document](https://document.airnow.gov/technical-assistance-document-for-the-reporting-of-daily-air-quality.pdf) describes how to calculate the AQI
based on pollutant concentration data.

### What do we need

- Data on following pollutants: PM2.5, PM10, O3, NO2, SO2, CO
- Datapoints with intervals of less than 8 hours (required) and ideally 1 hour (preferred)
